lunæ - Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

About lunæ

lunæ is a helper bot for Karuta, a Discord card collecting game. It provides utilities for managing card competitions and other Karuta-related activities on your Discord server.

Data Collection

lunæ may store the following data:

• Card images
• Owner Discord ID
• Card codes
• Card competition configurations (e.g., channel IDs, other settings)
• Karuta clan-related data

Use of Data

The collected data is used solely for providing features related to Karuta competitions and clan utilities. It will not be shared or sold to third parties.

Data Retention

Data will be retained as long as it is needed to operate lunæ’s features or until requested to be deleted by the server owner.

Data Deletion

If you are a Discord server owner or user and would like your data deleted, please contact the bot developer through the support server or bot profile.

Terms of Use

By inviting and using lunæ in your server, you agree not to use the bot for malicious purposes or to violate Discord's Terms of Service.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. By continuing to use lunæ after changes, you accept the updated policy.

For questions or concerns, please reach out through the support channels.
